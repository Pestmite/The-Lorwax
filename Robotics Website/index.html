About Royal West Academy

Royal West Academy is a school with a mission. Our teachers are committed to ensuring that every student thrives in a safe and supportive environment. Students have the opportunity to explore a variety of fields, including languages, sciences, technology, and the arts. Graduates leave with vital life skills such as leadership, independence, and self-discipline. Our school offers over 100 different extracurricular activities and clubs to take part in, thrilling field trips, and fun events like bake sales and dress-down days. At RWA, we go above and beyond to make sure our students reach for the stars.
